Global Formula Racing

As part of my Bachelor of Science senior capstone project, I joined the Global Formula Racing (GFR) team at OSU to contribute to the enhancement of their simulator. The project involved a multifaceted approach, combining a review of past projects, hands-on code editing, and research of tools to elevate the simulator's performance and functionality.

Final Oral Exam

For my Master of Science final oral exam project, I created a comprehensive presentation of Formula 1 using the power of R and RStudio. The focal point of my analysis revolved around drivers and circuits, employing the powerful ggplot(2) package to visually represent patterns and insights from the extensive dataset. The dataset encompassed information spanning from 1950 to 2023, providing details on drivers, circuits, constructors, championships, and more. By delving into this historical data, I aimed to discover trends, correlations, and significant factors that have shaped the world of Formula 1 over the decades.
